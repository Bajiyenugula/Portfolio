Development of Computer Vision Model for Estimating Size and Weight of Beetroot
Objective:

To develop a computer vision model that accurately estimates the size and weight of beetroot to improve the efficiency of sorting and processing in the food industry.

Procedure:

Utilized image processing techniques and machine learning algorithms to train a model on a dataset of beetroot images. The model was tested and validated for accuracy and precision.

Conclusion:

The developed model demonstrated high accuracy in estimating beetroot size and weight, leading to potential improvements in sorting and processing efficiency.

Development of Biodegradable Corn Husk Straw
Objective:

To create a sustainable alternative to plastic straws using corn husk, focusing on biodegradability and environmental impact.

Procedure:

Processed corn husks into straw material, tested for durability and biodegradability. Conducted environmental impact assessments to validate the benefits of the material.

Conclusion:

The corn husk straws were successfully developed, showing excellent biodegradability and a significant reduction in environmental impact compared to plastic straws.
